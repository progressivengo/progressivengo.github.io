About Progressive Delhi Society

Thanks for your interest in Progressive Delhi Society and as the President of this NGO I would like to welcome you to be a part of this initiative and help people and society and make this world a better one. We welcome you to take part in the activities of the NGO which are bringing smiles on the faces of the needy people.

Progressive Delhi Society was established in the Oct. 2002. society has been registered under the Society Registration Act of 1860 under Govt. of NCT of Delhi. Education & literacy, health & family welfare, information & communication technology, rural development & poverty alleviation, vocational training, women’s development & empowerment. We take pride in working for the upliftment of weaker sections of society in the following areas...

Our vision to establish, run, takeover or manage and maintain schools with on object to provide sound, nursery, primary, middle, secondary and senior secondary education to children, various training institutions in typing short hand computer, fine arts, modeling, yoga, and other professional training.

 

To conduct research in education and other disciplines on the different subjects relating to education.

 

To engage employee or line appropriated staff, workers, legal experts and other professionals, attorneys, managers and agents for the work and furtherance of the aims and objects of the society and to pay their wakes salaries, stipends or fees. Now we works to drug deadiction many areas in Delhi. espeacialy West Delhi through road shows, puppets show, etc.

 

Awereness programmes conducted under the guidance and supervision of Delhi Govt. Ministry of Women & Child.

 

To purchase/acquire land and/or building in the name of the society and undergo construction thereupon.

 

To provide and render help and assistance in cash or kind to poor, destitute people, widows etc.

 

To establish and maintain institution for the handicapped, blind, deaf and dumb, poor, scheduled caste and schedule tribes, minorities, backward classes and other needy people to provide the training.

 

To do best efforts for the promotion of research and education in medical science including surgery by running the various schools, colleges and other medical research and education institutes.

 

To collect information, notifications, policies, from the government, semi- government, national/ international agencies and NGOs
